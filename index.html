Overview

Godrej Avenue Eleven promises to redefine luxury living. It is a sprawling 3.85-acre freehold land parcel nestled amidst 2.4 acres of serene open spaces and a grand clubhouse. Impeccably designed to offer a lifestyle of unparalleled comfort and convenience, the project boasts a plethora of world-class amenities that cater to the needs of the modern-day urbanite. Godrej Avenue Eleven features an Olympic-length pool, a temperature-controlled pool, and an infinity-edge pool that offers a perfect retreat to relax and rejuvenate. An observatory has been set up for astronomy enthusiasts to explore the wonders of the universe. The grand clubhouse features a lounge, business center, and banquet hall for hosting events and social gatherings. At Godrej Avenue Eleven, meticulously landscaped gardens, a state-of-the-art gym, badminton courts, squash courts, tennis cum multiplay court, and a half-futsal court provide ample opportunities to stay active and fit.

The modern layouts with a timeless touch offer customizable plans that ensure ample sunlight and excellent cross-ventilation, making each residence a haven of tranquility. Privacy is a top priority, and each residence features an exclusive entryway, no shared walls, and a dedicated staff/delivery entrance, ensuring enhanced privacy and security. Godrej Avenue Eleven Mahalaxmi offers an unparalleled blend of luxury, comfort, and privacy, which makes it an ideal choice for those who seek an exclusive lifestyle. Experience luxury living and indulge in the finest amenities and facilities Godrej Avenue Eleven offers.
